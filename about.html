Why this project exists?

N for E is a simple learning website built to make brain science easier to understand. The goal is to explain ideas clearly, avoid unnecessary vocabulary when possible, and help curious students build confidence.

Even if someone currently doesn't have the exact vocabulary or the most perfect mind, they clearly have the potential to learn and think big, rather than limit their own curiosity.

The project will grow into a space for neuroscience basics, bigger questions, and simple explanations that make the subject feel less intimidating.

Built for curious learners.
